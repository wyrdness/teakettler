## Overview

The Teakettler is a small armored creature from lumberjack folklore that runs backwards at high speed while making teakettle whistling sounds and emitting steam. Used to explain mysterious forest sounds.

---

## Characteristics

- **Size**: Cat-sized
- **Armor**: Covered in scales
- **Behavior**: Runs backwards constantly
- **Sound**: Teakettle whistling
- **Steam**: Emits from mouth while running

---

## Purpose

Humorous explanation for mysterious whistling sounds in forests (actually wind, birds, or natural steam vents).

---

## Status: Folklore

Never meant to be believed - pure entertainment.
